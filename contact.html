Frequently Asked Questions

What's your development approach?

I keep things practical: figure out what matters, ship a solid first version, then improve it with feedback. I also care a lot about reliability and debuggability.

Are you open to collabs?

Yep. If it’s a fun problem (or something genuinely useful), I’m happy to chat and see if it’s a good fit.

Can you help with existing projects?

Yep. I can jump into an existing codebase, fix bugs, improve performance, and add features without turning it into a rewrite.

What makes you different from other developers?

I’m easy to work with, I communicate clearly, and I care about the boring details that keep things running. The goal is simple: ship something that works and is easy to maintain.
